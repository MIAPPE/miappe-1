Steering commitee

Development of MIAPPE is currently overseen by a steering committee whose members represent a variety of interested countries, institutes and projects. Members of this committee can also be active developers of the standard. The current committee comprises:
  • Elizabeth Arnaud (Bioversity International, Montpellier, France)
  • Paul Kersey (Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, United Kingdom)
  • Paweł Krajewski (Institute of Plant Genetics, Polish Academy of Sciences, Poznań, Poland)
  • Matthias Lange (Leibniz-Institut Für Pflanzengenetik und Kulturpflanzenforschung, Gatersleben, Germany)
  • Cyril Pommier (INRAE, Versailles, France)
  • Bjoern Usadel (Forschungszentrum Jülich, Germany)
  • Noah Fahlgren (Danforth Plant Science Center, United States of America)
Among the current projects of the steering committee is the development of a full governance mechanism including the election of future versions of the committee. Minutes of the committee's meetings are published on GitHub; read them here.

Join the MIAPPE community

To be informed of new developments in MIAPPE, please consider subscribing to the MIAPPE mailing list. Traffic is low, but it subscribers are informed of future meetings, proposed revisions to the standard, and new tools.
